## 🎮 **Blocking vs Non-Blocking Enemy Attacks**

### 🔒 **BLOCKING (Original - `asyncEnemyTurn = false`)**

**What "blocking" means:**
- The code **waits** for the enemy turn to completely finish before continuing
- Your game is "blocked" from accepting new input until enemy is done

**Timeline:**
```
YOU MAKE A MATCH
    ↓
Gems disappear, fall, spawn (you're locked out)
    ↓
Player attacks enemy (350ms animation)
    ↓
⏸️  WAIT - Nothing happens (400ms pause)
    ↓
Enemy attacks you (350ms animation)
    ↓
⏸️  BOARD STILL LOCKED
    ↓
✅ NOW you can make your next match! (board unlocks)
```

**Total wait:** ~1100ms from when cascades finish until you can swap again

[Continued with detailed blocking explanation...]

### ⚡ **NON-BLOCKING (New - `asyncEnemyTurn = true`)**

**What "non-blocking" means:**
- The code **starts** the enemy turn in the background (using `Task {}`)
- Your game immediately unlocks and continues
- Enemy animations still play, but don't stop you from preparing next move
